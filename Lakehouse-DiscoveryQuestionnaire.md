# Lake House Rental Web App — Discovery Questionnaire

_All questions are optional._

## Guest Account & Booking Flow

- Will guests need accounts, or should they be able to book as a guest?
- Walk me through what you imagine a guest doing from arriving on the website to completing a reservation.
- Should reservations require owner approval before confirming booking or should it be instant?
- Should guests be able to cancel their reservation online?

## Property Rules & Policies

- Are there property-specific rules guests must agree to? If so, what are they?
- Are there restrictions on who can book?
  - Minimum age
  - Maximum guests
  - Pets
  - Events/parties
- What are your cancellation policies?
- Are there liability waivers or agreements?
- Do guests need to sign anything before staying?
- Are there local regulations or rental restrictions?
- Are there specific requirements around security deposits?

## Availability & Scheduling

- Do you want to manage availability and control dates?
- Do you want to be able to block dates?
- Are same-day reservations allowed?
- Are check-in/check-out times fixed or do they vary?

## Payments & Fees

- How should guests pay?
- Should payment happen during booking?
- Do you require full payment upfront or a deposit?
- How much do you want to charge per night?
- Are there additional fees?
  - Cleaning fee
  - Service fee
  - Pet fee
- What happens if a guest cancels?
- Should refunds happen automatically?

## Pricing Rules

- Do different seasons have different pricing?
- Are there special rates for holidays or events?

## Property Details

- What are the lake house amenities?
- What are the nearby attractions?

## Client Budget & Project Spend

- What is your approximate budget range for this project?
- How much are you looking to spend on this project? (specific target)
- Is this a one-time build budget or does it include ongoing costs?
- Any budget constraints, phasing, or priorities we should know?
